Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Combat Performance IQ ("the App") is developed and operated by MJB Photos. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only the information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Athlete profile information (name, weight class, gym/team)
  • Training logs, sparring sessions, and performance data you enter
  • Fight camp and wrestling season data
  • Opponent scouting notes and game plans
  • Readiness and wellness check-in data
  • Nutrition and weight tracking entries

2. HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, the App may access HealthKit data including heart rate, sleep analysis, active energy, and body mass. This data is used solely to display your readiness metrics within the App. HealthKit data is never sold, shared with third parties, or used for advertising. HealthKit data is stored on your device and in your personal iCloud account only.

3. Camera and Photo Library

The App requests camera and photo library access to allow you to upload training footage for AI video analysis. Video files are processed temporarily and are not stored on our servers. All video analysis is performed via the Anthropic Claude API and is not retained after the session ends.

4. iCloud and CloudKit

Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service. MJB Photos does not have access to your iCloud data. Data sync between devices uses your Apple ID and is governed by Apple's iCloud Privacy Policy.

5. AI Features (Corner AI)

When you use Corner AI, your questions and relevant training context are sent to the Anthropic Claude API to generate responses. This data is transmitted securely over HTTPS. We do not store your Corner AI conversations on our servers. Please review Anthropic's Privacy Policy at anthropic.com/privacy for information on how they handle API data.

6. Subscriptions and Purchases

All subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. MJB Photos does not collect or store payment information. Purchase history is managed through your Apple ID.

7. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your location
  • We do not collect device identifiers for advertising
  • We do not share your data with advertisers
  • We do not sell your personal data to any third party
  • We do not use your data to build advertising profiles

8. Data Storage and Security

All personal data is stored locally on your device and in your personal iCloud account. Data transmitted to the Anthropic API for AI features is encrypted in transit via HTTPS. We do not operate our own servers that store your personal data.

9. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

10. Your Rights

You can delete all data stored by the App at any time by deleting the App from your device. To delete iCloud data associated with the App, go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage and remove the App's data.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
